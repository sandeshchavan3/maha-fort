Birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

  • Shivneri Fort is located in the Junnar region of Pune district.
  • It is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, born in 1630.
  • The fort was strategically designed to guard important trade routes.
  • It features seven gates with strong fortifications and bastions.
  • The fort has a temple of Goddess Shivai Devi, after whom Shivaji was named.
  • There is a water pond called Badami Talav at the center of the fort.
  • Shivneri was under the rule of the Yadavas, Bahamanis, Mughals, and Marathas.
